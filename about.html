WANTOK GARDENING is a professional manufacturer and trader for the gardening tools and hand tools which was established in 1993. The main products include all kinds of Rakes, Scissors, Spades, Saws and other dozens of categories, tens of thousands of kinds of tools. WANTOK is devoting itself to making labor an easier thing.

As a professional manufacturer, WANTOK has passed the BSCI, ICS, FSC and other professional inspection strict inspection. Perfect management system and quality system is the foundation for the development of the factory. At present, WANTOK has its own supply chain and warehouses in Shandong, Hebei and Zhejiang provinces of China.

WANTOK attaches great importance to talent construction and actively trains up its own R&D team. At present, three products systems have been formed, and more than 10,000 products are sold worldwide every year.

WANTOK is attractive to customers in different regions. In the past two decades, more than 300 professional customers all over the world have cooperated with WANTOK.

Headquartered in Shanghai Oriental global enterprise park, the company provides one-stop procurement services for global customers: ordering, inspection, packaging supervision, after-sales and other worry-free services.
