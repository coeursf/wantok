WANTOK GARDENING — Tools That Make Work Easier

Founded in 1993, WANTOK GARDENING is a leading manufacturer and exporter specializing in gardening tools, farming tools, hand tools, and garden accessories. With tens of thousands of SKUs across rakes, scissors, spades, saws, and more, we provide a complete and reliable product range for retailers, importers, wholesalers, and distributors worldwide.

For more than 30 years, our mission has remained simple: to make outdoor work easier, safer, and more enjoyable. Every WANTOK tool is engineered with durability, comfort, and practicality in mind—helping both professionals and home gardeners achieve better results with less effort.

Manufacturing Excellence

WANTOK's manufacturing strength is built on strict quality systems, advanced production lines, and a deeply experienced engineering team.

  • Certified & Compliant: Our facilities have passed BSCI, ICS, FSC, and ISO9001 inspections.
  • Modern Production: Automated equipment, precision machining, advanced heat treatments, and strict surface finishing processes ensure consistent durability.
  • Rigorous Quality Control: Material selection, manufacturing stages, and final inspections are tightly supervised at every step.
  • Strategic Supply Chain: Warehouses and production support centers across Shandong, Hebei, and Zhejiang Province ensure efficient logistics and stable supply.

This foundation allows us to deliver tools that meet the high standards of global markets while supporting the scale and timelines required by global customers.

Design & Innovation

In a competitive global market, innovation is essential. WANTOK invests heavily in talent development and product R&D, maintaining a dedicated design team that continuously brings fresh solutions to the industry.

Over the past 30 years, we have developed nearly 26 complete product series and hold numerous independent intellectual property rights and appearance patents. Today, more than 10,000 WANTOK products reach customers worldwide each year, reinforcing our reputation for quality and reliability in the global landscaping and hardware market.

Global Market Leadership

WANTOK has established long-term partnerships with over 300 professional customers worldwide, including international building-material chains, supermarkets, importers, and wholesalers. Our commitment to consistent quality and reliable service has earned us high recognition in global markets that prioritize dependability, safety, and performance.

Many well-known customers continue to choose WANTOK not only for product quality, but for our operational strength—stable supply chain, flexible production, transparent communication, and strict compliance with international standards.

Trusted Sales & Service

Headquartered in Shanghai Oriental Global Enterprise Park, WANTOK provides seamless one-stop procurement services—from ordering and packaging supervision to after-sales support.

  • Our professional team is experienced in international trade rules and familiar with manufacturing details.
  • Every shipment undergoes pre-shipment inspection by WANTOK's internal QC team.
  • We maintain long-term cooperation with TÜV, BV, SGS and other respected third-party institutions for added reliability.

We believe excellent service is not a promise—it's a discipline. With every order, we aim to provide an easy, secure, and efficient purchasing experience.
